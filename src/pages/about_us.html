Welcome to the modern metal art smithy.

Alhidad is a manufacturer of metal artworks and structural steel in Ukraine.

In our catalogue you will find unique structural steelwork, ready-made forged items, and will be able to acquire one of a kind forged items for any of your projects.

Metal art smithy, Alhidad, had been on the market for more than 20 years. During this time we acquired rich in depth experience in the manufacture of highly sophisticated forged metal items.
