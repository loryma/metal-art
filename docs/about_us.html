Welcome to the website of Modern Metal Art.

What started out as a niche type of hobby — creating metal art — blosommed into a full fledged operation.

This happened after the need for steelworks was evident by growing number of diverse product requests.

Metal Art Master has built a network of strong relationships within the industry that allow us to work on a range of different projects. We value both our new and regular clients.

We have extensive experience in all types of steelwork – structural steel for industrial and commercial structures, industrial retro-fit expansions.

What binds together every member of the Metal Art Master family is a shared passion for metal artworks, shaped by value-based guiding principles.
